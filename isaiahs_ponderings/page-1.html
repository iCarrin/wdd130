All dimensions are the previous dimension just copied infinitely on a 90-degree right angle. A bunch of lines lined up together is a plan, a bunch of planes stacked on top of each other is space (not a cube because that’s not infinite). So logically the 0th dimension is a point. A point has no breadth width or height, and an infinite amount of points lined up together are a line. (boom)

Space can’t be multiplied at any other 90-degree angle because it’s taking up all the room, so what if we took a picture of it and lined those pictures up end to end and made a line with them making an infinite line, a timeline! You could then go on to a timeline a time plane and a time-space, but after some years I realized my error. These are spaces, not things.
